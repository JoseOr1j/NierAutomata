YorHa unit: 2B

2B a YorHa unit type battilion. She is more offensive than her partner, 9S. Her mission is bring an end to the alien lifeforms that have taken over the earth. She is more serious than other types.

"The machines don't have feelings, you said it yourself Nines"

2B Nier Automata

YorHa unit: 9S

9S is a YorHa unit type scanner. He is less offensive than other YorHa units. His main use is that he finds and analyzes data rather purely combat like his companion 2B. He is more analytical than other types.

"The Commander, Me.. 2B? Sacrificial lambs. All of us. Isn't that HILARIOUS?"

9S Nier Automata
Yorha unit: A2

A2 is a special type of YorHa unit, shes a rouge older version of a YorHa unit. She shares ties with 2B, and knows about a secret that changes 9S' outlook of YorHa, The Council of Humanity, and the "humans" on the Moon.

"I never quite realized how beautiful this world is"

A2 Nier Automata

Adam

Adam is the brother of Eve, and is a strange YorHa unit. He was born practically out of the android lifeforms and represents a YorHa unit. He also represents one the first peoples from the bible, Adam and Eve.

"Machines are weapons capable of evolution. We can grow, we can become aware"

Adam Nier Automata

Eve

Eve, the brother of Adam. Eve also has a similar apperance to a YorHa unit, he was born from a part of his brother Adam. Eventually Adam is killed off by 2B and 9S which causes Eve to go beserk and attack YorHa units 2B and 9S and clash.

"My name? This book says it's a woman's name. Shouldn't we be called Cain and Abel or something?"

Eve Nier Automata
